HTML, or HyperText Markup Language, was created by Sir Tim Berners-Lee in 1991. Berners-Lee, a British computer scientist, is widely recognized as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He developed HTML as a way to share and structure documents on the web, laying the foundation for modern web development. While the initial version was HTML 1.0, the first standardized version, HTML 2.0, was published in 1995.
